Home Gymnastic Apparatus Project

Engineering Strategies and Practice II | Group Project

  16/4/2024

During the second semester course APS112, I worked closely with my group and our client to develop
a gymnastic apparatus that could be deployed safely and easily at the clients home.

We presented our top design, the LassoR to the teaching team and to the client to persuade them
into accepting the design!

For the project, I helped define the Measures of Success and gathered experimental data by
testing our prototype in real life!



After the tests, I estimated the lifetime of the design!


Overall, I learned about the engineering design process and further improved a lot of my
communication and team work skills!

NutriCulture Hydroponics Prototype

Prototype render created during the SHAD summer program

  24/7/2022

The prototype showcases the vision of a hydroponic system that can be utilized in northern areas of Canada,
to improve food sovereignty.
Worked closely with the members of my Design Entrepreneurship project group, to make the prototype
accurate, including the view of the cargo crate exterior to protect against the elements,
The insulation layer which helps maintain growing temperatures,
and the hydroponic system which produces plants and filters water.
